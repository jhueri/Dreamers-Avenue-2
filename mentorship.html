Dreamers Avenue Mentorship Program

The Dreamers Avenue Mentorship Program empowers undocumented students by providing personalized guidance and support throughout the college application process.

Find a Mentor

How It Works:

This mentorship program is completely free of cost to ensure that every student has the opportunity to succeed without financial barriers.

  • Personalized Mentor Matching: We match mentees with mentors based on factors such as their intended college major, career interests, and personal preferences to ensure a supportive and productive relationship.
  • One-on-One Guidance: Mentors provide tailored support to mentees through every step of their college journey. This includes navigating complex college applications, identifying scholarship opportunities, offering academic advice, and providing emotional encouragement.
  • Flexible Communication: Mentors and mentees can connect virtually through various platforms, including email, video calls, or other online tools, ensuring regular and meaningful interactions that fit into both schedules. Our program is designed to accommodate busy lives while maintaining impactful engagement.

Eligibility:

Mentees

  • Must be an undocumented student residing in the U.S. who is currently in high school or an incoming college freshman.
  • Looking for personalized guidance with college applications, including navigating financial aid and scholarship opportunities.
  • Committed to actively participating in mentorship sessions and utilizing provided resources.

Eligibility:

Mentors

  • Should have personal or professional experience with the college application process.
  • Must understand the unique barriers undocumented students face when pursuing higher education, such as legal, financial, and emotional challenges.
  • Willing to dedicate time to offering one-on-one support, sharing resources, and fostering a safe and inclusive environment for mentees.

Why You Should Be a Mentor

  • Make a Lasting Impact: Your guidance can help mentees overcome obstacles and gain confidence in their journey toward higher education.
  • Grow as a Leader: Mentoring hones your communication and leadership skills while offering you a chance to learn from your mentee.
  • Be Part of a Mission-Driven Network: Join a community of like-minded individuals passionate about education and equity.

Time Commitment

  • Meet virtually with your mentee at least 1-2 times a month.
  • Dedicate time to providing guidance on college applications, scholarships, and personal development.
  • *This is a volunteer position and not paid.
