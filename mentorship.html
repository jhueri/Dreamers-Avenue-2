Dreamers Avenue Mentorship Program

The Dreamers Avenue Mentorship Program empowers undocumented students by providing personalized guidance and support throughout the college application process.

Find a Mentor

How it works

At Dreamers Avenue, we understand the unique challenges that undocumented students face when applying to college. Our mentorship program is designed to guide and support mentees throughout the college application process, ensuring they feel empowered and equipped with the resources they need to succeed.

  • Personalized Mentor Matching: We carefully match each mentee with a mentor based on their needs, goals, and intended college major or field of interest.
  • One-on-One Guidance: Mentors work directly with mentees to help them navigate college applications, find scholarship opportunities, and offer academic guidance and emotional support.
  • Flexible Communication: We believe in making mentorship as accessible and convenient as possible. Mentors and mentees communicate virtually, using a variety of platforms such as email, video calls, and online messaging. This flexibility ensures that mentors and mentees can maintain regular communication, regardless of geographical location or busy schedules.

Eligibility:

Mentees

  • Must be an undocumented student residing in the U.S. who is currently in high school or an incoming college freshman.
  • Looking for personalized guidance with college applications, including navigating financial aid and scholarship opportunities.
  • Committed to actively participating in mentorship sessions and utilizing provided resources.

Mentors

  • Should have personal or professional experience with the college application process.
  • Must understand the unique barriers undocumented students face when pursuing higher education, such as legal, financial, and emotional challenges.
  • Willing to dedicate time to offering one-on-one support, sharing resources, and fostering a safe and inclusive environment for mentees.
